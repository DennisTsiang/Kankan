What is Kankan?

Kankan is a project management system based on the use of a kanban.

Kankan offers a an easy way to make sure you meet all of your deadlines for any kind of tasks. You can share your projects with friends and co-workers easily and control which tasks they are assigned to!

Who is Kankan for?

Kankan is for everyone who has something to do. You could be a teacher, student, developer or even just someone who has chores to do, we can help you manage your work.

Developers

Kankan offers support for github, here you can link in a gitbug repository andconnect to all of your files and different functions in your code.
